1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Name, email, and message content when you submit our contact form
  • Any information you voluntarily provide when commenting or entering contests

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visit, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data
  • Device data: device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: how you interact with our Site, which articles you read, and features you use
  • General geographic location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)

c) Information from Third Parties

  • Analytics data from Google Analytics
  • Advertising data from Google AdSense and its network partners
  • Social media interaction data if you use our sharing buttons

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and maintain our website and its content
  • To send our newsletter (only with your explicit consent)
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our website
  • To serve personalized and contextual advertising through Google AdSense
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Site.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and partners to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Social Media Cookies: Set when you interact with embedded social content or sharing buttons.

You may opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices or www.youronlinechoices.eu. You may also instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, though some Site features may be affected.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense, a web advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site or other websites on the internet.

Google AdSense may collect and use information including your IP address and browser information, browsing behavior across websites using Google services, demographic information and inferred interests, and the ads you click and your interaction with them.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information on how Google collects and processes data, visit https://policies.google.com/privacy.

We comply with Google's Publisher Policies and AdSense Program Policies. All advertising content on this Site is clearly presented and does not interfere with user experience or navigation.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track and report website traffic. Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. We have enabled IP anonymization on our Google Analytics implementation. The data is aggregated and used to improve our content and user experience.

You may opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites, plugins, and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies. These may include:

  • Google (Analytics, AdSense, Fonts): Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram/Meta: Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Pinterest: Pinterest Privacy Policy
  • YouTube: YouTube Privacy
  • Amazon Associates: We may participate in the Amazon Associates program and earn commissions from qualifying purchases.

7. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted companies that assist us in operating our website, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, acquisition, or sale of our assets
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for any other purpose

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction: Request we limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing

California Residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and the right to non-discrimination.

EEA Residents (GDPR): Our legal bases for processing include your consent, the performance of a contract, our legitimate interests, and compliance with legal obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@wanderlustchronicles.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information promptly. We do not display personalized advertising to users we know to be under 18 years of age.

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit, secure server infrastructure, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy. Newsletter subscriber information is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 2 years. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention periods (typically 14 months for individual data).

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside your jurisdiction. We are based in Canada and process data there. Where required by law, we ensure appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place for cross-border data transfers.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Changes are effective when posted.
