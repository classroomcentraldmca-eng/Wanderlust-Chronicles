Our Story

We Are Wanderlust Chronicles

Born from a suitcase and a camera, Wanderlust Chronicles began in 2018 as a personal travel journal kept by our founder, Alex Mercer, during a year-long trip through Southeast Asia. What started as a way to remember details for friends back home slowly grew into something larger: a resource other travelers began finding useful, then relying on, then requesting more of.

Today we are a small team of passionate explorers, writers, and photographers committed to inspiring authentic travel experiences worldwide. We believe travel changes people — it broadens perspective, builds empathy, and reminds us of our shared humanity across vast cultural distance. Every article we publish is guided by that philosophy: honest, thorough, and designed to genuinely help you travel better.

Editorial Standards

How We Research & Write

Every destination guide begins with real time on the ground — a minimum of one to two weeks per major guide, often longer for larger countries. We don't rely on press trips exclusively; when we do accept one, we disclose it, and our itinerary and opinions remain entirely our own regardless of what a tourism board hoped we'd write.

Prices, opening hours, and logistics are checked against official sources and updated when we learn they've changed. If we make a mistake, we correct it — and we welcome readers flagging outdated information through our contact page. Affiliate links, where present, are clearly disclosed and never influence which destinations, hotels, or experiences we choose to cover.
